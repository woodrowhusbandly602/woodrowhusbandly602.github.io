Run the rooftops of a sleeping city. Shadow Dash is a one-thumb auto-runner — tap to leap, hold to glide, release to drop through the shadows. Every level is a single handcrafted route with one perfect line through it.

No ads, no energy meters, no interruptions. Just you, the moonlight, and a clean run.

  • 60 handcrafted levels across 5 districts
  • One-thumb controls — play anywhere
  • Ghost replays of your best runs
  • Zero ads, ever
