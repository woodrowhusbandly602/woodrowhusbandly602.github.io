Privacy Policy

EFFECTIVE 2026-07-09

Overview

Tatami Trials is made by NinjaApps. This policy describes what data Tatami Trials handles — which, by design, is very little.

Data collection

Tatami Trials does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising identifiers, and no tracking of any kind.

Any progress and settings are stored locally on your device (and in your personal iCloud backup, if you have one), where NinjaApps has no access to them.

Third-party services

Tatami Trials uses Apple Game Center for optional leaderboards. Game Center is operated by Apple and governed by Apple's privacy policy. Beyond that, the app embeds no third-party SDKs and shares no data with anyone.

Children's privacy

Tatami Trials does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If a future update changes what data the app handles, this policy will be updated before that version ships. The effective date above always reflects the current version.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be raised through the app's App Store page.
